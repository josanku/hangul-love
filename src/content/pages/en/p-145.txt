    Human, the center of sound

    The initial sound represents movement and emergence,
    signifying the work of Heaven. The final sound symbolizes
    stopping and remaining, reflecting the work of Earth.
    The medial sound connects the emergence of the initial
    sound to the completion of the final sound, making it whole.
    This is the role of Human.
    At the core of written sounds lies the medial sound, which
    harmonizes the initial and final sounds to form a complete
    syllable. For example, in the words 몸 Mom and 맘 Mam,
    both share the same initial and final sounds, yet their
    pronunciation changes due to the medial sound.
    This principle reflects the idea that Heaven and Earth create
    all things, but it is Humanity that gives them meaning,
    balance, and purpose.
