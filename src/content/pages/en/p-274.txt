7. Philosophy terms in Korean

    Hasattang Balk-I-Eo Daum( or Hattangsa Eo-I-Balk
    Daum) is a simplified and intuitive Korean expression for the
    core principles of Samjae (Three Element Philosophy) and
    Yin-Yang and the Five Elements, which are fundamental to
    the creation of Hunminjeongeum. Although Yin-Yang and
    the Five Elements are key concepts in Eastern philosophy
    that explain the principles of the universe and nature, their
    Chinese-based terminology can create linguistic barriers for
    modern generations, making them seem difficult and
    unfamiliar.
    Moreover, the Samjae (Heaven, Earth, and Human)
    philosophy is not only a central cosmological view for the
    Korean people but also the most essential concept in
    Hunminjeongeum. Therefore, expressing it in native Korean
    terms became even more necessary.
