Earth, the balance and stability of sounds

    ㅡ (eu) represents Earth. It is pronounced with the tongue
    slightly curled but more relaxed than ㆍ, producing a sound
    that is neither deep nor shallow. This reflects the balance
    and stability of Earth.
    The horizontal shape of ㅡ symbolizes the flatness and
    expansiveness of Earth, which provides the foundation for
    all living things. It represents the harmony and balance that
    Earth maintains between Heaven and Human.
    ㅡ corresponds to the time just before dawn, around 2 a.m.,
    when the night ends, and Earth begins to awaken. This is
    the time when energy and life start to stir, marking the
    beginning of activity on Earth.
    The sound of ㅡ is neutral and balanced, positioned between
    the deep sound of ㆍ Heaven and the shallow sound of ㅣ
    Human. It symbolizes equilibrium and harmony, reflecting
    the role of Earth as the mediator between Heaven and
    Human.
