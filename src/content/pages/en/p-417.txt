This work translates the Hunminjeongeum Haeryebon into English.
    Rather than focusing on specialized linguistic explanations, the emphasis is
    placed on conveying the principles and philosophy of its creation from a
    general perspective.
    I hope it inspires more questions and curiosity.
    Interpretation was made with a sense of flexibility, and various opinions
    and imaginations were included.
    The achievements of many scholars were utilized.
    If you find any missing source citations, please let me know.
    Various AI tools were also used in the process.
    This work will be shared, improved, and continuously updated with
    collective feedback.
    I hope this brings us a little closer to understanding King Sejong's grand
    philosophy and the profound principles behind the creation of Hangul.
