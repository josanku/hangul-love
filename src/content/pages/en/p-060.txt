HANGUL, Dream · Essence · Light of Korean

    Kkum · Eol · Bit is a conceptual model that portrays Hangul
    not just as a writing system, but as the embodiment of the
    Korean people's long-held dream (꿈 Kkum), condensed into
    a cultural and philosophical seed (얼 Eol), and ultimately
    radiating as light (빛 Bit) for all humanity.
    The creation of Hunminjeongeum by King Sejong The Great
    was not merely a royal act, but a cultural transformation
    rooted in collective needs, cultural capacity, and
    philosophical depth. Today, we are called to carry this vision
    forward — to complete it through a new standard:
    Jeongeum Hangul, the new standard based on
    Hunminjeongeum, for all of Human beings.
