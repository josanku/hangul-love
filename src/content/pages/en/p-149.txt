The ultimate sounds

    Ah!
    With the creation of Jeongeum,
    the principles of Heaven,
    Earth,
    and all things
    are perfectly embodied.

    How truly mysterious this is!

    Surely,
    it was the Heavens
    that opened the mind
    of the wise King Sejong the Great
    and achieved this
    through his hands.
