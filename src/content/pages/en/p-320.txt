    Hangul is more than a writing system. It is a linguistic
    philosophy, a scientifically designed script, and a tool finely
    tuned for the digital age. With its unique adaptability and
    structural clarity, Hangul serves as both the foundation and
    catalyst for cultural creativity. It fuels the expansion of
    Hallyu across the globe. Jeong Inji once predicted that the
    invention of Hunminjeongeum would open a new world of
    light—today, that vision is being realized among the citizens
    of the entire world.
