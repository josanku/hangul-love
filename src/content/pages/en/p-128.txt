Numbers of medial sounds

    The medial sounds are deeply connected to the principles of
    creation and the cosmic order of numbers. They reflect the
    generative process of Heaven and Earth and the numerical
    order governing Daum.


    First creation with single ㆍ

    ㅗ (o) is the first sound originating from Heaven, associated
    with the number 1, which represents Water.
    ㅏ (a) is the second sound from Heaven, associated with the
    number 3, representing Wood.
    ㅜ (u) is the first sound originating from Earth, associated
    with the number 2, representing Fire.
    ㅓ(eo) is the second sound from Earth, associated with the
    number 4, representing Metal.
