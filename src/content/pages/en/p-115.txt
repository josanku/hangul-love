    ㅜ (u) makes a sound similar to ㅡ (eu) but is pronounced
    with the lips rounded. Its shape is formed by combining ㅡ
    Earth and ㆍ Heaven, symbolizing the first meeting of
    Heaven and Earth. It represents the image of the sun setting
    below the horizon, reflecting the cycle of completion and
    renewal.

    ㅓ (eo) makes a sound similar to ㅡ (eu) but is pronounced
    with the lips spread open. Its shape is formed by combining
    ㆍ Heaven and ㅣ Human, symbolizing the intervention of
    Human in the creation of all things. Just like ㅏ (a), it
    reflects the principle that Human plays a crucial role in
    connecting and completing the creations of Heaven and
    Earth.
