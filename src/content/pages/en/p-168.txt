Jeongeum bestowed by Heaven

    Though Jeongeum consists
    of only 28 characters,
    a deeper examination reveals
    the profound and mysterious principles
    embedded within.

    Despite its deep-rooted meaning
    and intricate principles,
    Jeongeum is designed
    to be easy to learn and understand
    for all people.

    Jeongeum is not something
    that can be merely created
    by human effort.

    It is a perfect sound system
    bestowed by Heaven.
