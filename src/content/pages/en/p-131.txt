Number of ㆍ ㅡ and the infinite of ㅣ

    ㆍ (Hanul-A [ʌ]) carries the number 5 of Heaven,
    representing the birth of Earth.
    ㅡ (eu) carries the number 10 of Earth, symbolizing the
    completion of Earth.
    These two vowels connect Heaven and Earth, grounding the
    cycle of creation and representing the origin and completion
    of all things.

    ㅣ (Human) is the only vowel without a numerical value or
    position.
    This reflects the unique role of Human as the core of cosmic
    harmony.
    Human is the mysterious link that unites BalkIEo,
    embodying the central balance of the universe.
    Unlike the other vowels, ㅣ does not belong to a specific
    place or number because Human is the harmonizer of all
    elements, transcending fixed order.
    Through this numerical and cosmic order, the medial sounds
    embody the principles of BalkIEo, Daum, and the directions
    of space.
