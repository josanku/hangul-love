Hangul creation are based on a deep philosophical thought
    of the Korean people that connects the universe and
    humanity, transcending a simple writing system.
    In this way, both Hangul and the Cheonbugyeong are based
    on the Samgeuk principle and share the human-centered
    philosophy that Human is Heaven. It is no coincidence that
    the philosophy of the Korean people, passed down for
    thousands of years, had a profound influence on King
    Sejong's creation of Hangul. These two great cultural
    heritages both embody the spirit of our nation that values
    humanity most within the order of the cosmos.
