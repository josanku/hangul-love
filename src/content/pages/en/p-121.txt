Placement of ㆍ and BalkIEo balance

    In the vowels ㅗ ㅏ ㅛ ㅑ(o a yo ya), the dot (ㆍ) is
    positioned above or to the outer right, symbolizing its
    emergence from Heaven and its association with Balkeum.
    Conversely, in the vowels ㅜ ㅓ ㅠ ㅕ(u eo yu yeo), the dot
    (ㆍ) is positioned below or to the inner left, signifying its
    emergence from Earth and its association with Eodum.

    This illustrates that the medial sounds are not merely
    phonetic components but embody a harmonious principle,
    Hattangsa, reflecting the interplay of Heaven Earth Human
    influence. They encapsulate the balance of cosmic forces,
    demonstrating that Hangul's structure is deeply rooted in the
    fundamental principles of nature and existence.
