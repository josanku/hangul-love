Initial sounds reflecting Daum

    Human sounds originate from Daum(the Five Movements,
    the Five Elements) in nature. Because of this, they naturally
    align with the cycle of the four seasons and harmonize with
    the five musical notes.

    The throat is deep and moist, corresponding to Water
    among Daum. Throat sounds are hollow and resonate
    clearly, just like water flows smoothly and transparently.
    They represent Winter and correspond to the musical note
    La.

    The molar sounds, produced at the back of the mouth
    where the molars are aligned, correspond to Wood. These
    sounds have a defined shape, similar to trees growing from
    water. They represent Spring and correspond to the musical
    note Mi.

    The tongue moves swiftly and sharply, producing tongue
    sounds that represent Fire. These sounds roll and spread out,
    like flames blazing and expanding.
    They correspond to Summer and the musical note Sol.
