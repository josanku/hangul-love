Significance of ㆍ being present in all vowels

    The fact that ㆍ(Hanul-A [ʌ]) is present in all eight vowels
    represents the principle of Balkeum’s bright energy
    spreading through the darkness of Eodum, permeating all
    things in the world and bringing about transformation.
    This reflects the cosmic flow of energy, where light emerges
    from darkness, symbolizing creation and change.


    Role of Human and the harmony of BalkIEo

    The compound vowels ㅛ ㅑ ㅠ ㅕ(yo ya yu yeo) all include
    ㅣ Human, signifying that Human plays a central role in
    harmonizing ㆍ Heaven and ㅡ Earth, and BalkEo.
    This symbolizes Human as the bridge and balance between
    the cosmic forces, reflecting the philosophy that Human is
    the master of all creation.

    Fulfillment of the principle with ㆍ as its root
    The medial sounds are designed to resemble the forms of
    Heaven, Earth, and Human, perfectly embodying the
    principle of Hattangsa(Samjae: the Three Principle).
