Easy and practical

    With Jeongeum, a wise person can master it in just half a
    day, while even those who learn slowly can become
    proficient within ten days. Using this writing system, one
    can interpret Hanja texts and understand their meanings
    clearly. It is also useful for recording legal documents and
    court cases, allowing readers to fully grasp the details and
    context. This makes Hangul not only easy to learn but also
    highly practical in everyday life and official matters.
