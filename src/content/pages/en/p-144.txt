    Daum(Daseot Umjikim: the Five Movements, The Five
    Elements) manifest as divine movements in Heaven and as
    material foundations on Earth.
    In Heaven, they appear as the dynamic actions of the divine,
    such as weather and natural phenomena, representing the
    source of change.

    In Earth, they form the material basis of existence, like
    growing trees and physical forms, where change becomes
    manifest. Human acts as the harmonizer between these
    cosmic forces. Through Human’s role in balancing BalkIEo,
    the changes initiated by Heaven are materialized and
    completed in Earth.
