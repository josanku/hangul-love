Harmony ofㆍㅡ ㅣ


    The vowel ㅗ (o)
    originates from Heaven
    and is pronounced
    by rounding the lips.

    Its shape harmonizes
    the roundness of Heaven (ㆍ) [ʌ]
    with the flatness of Earth (ㅡ) (eu).
    It resembles the image
    of the sun rising above the horizon.

    Similarly, the vowel ㅏ(a)
    also originates from Heaven,
    but it is pronounced
    by spreading the lips.


    This represents how things
    become revealed through Humans,
    symbolizing manifestation.
