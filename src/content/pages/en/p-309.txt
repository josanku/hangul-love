Hunminjeongeum must be re reconstructed in a modern
    context. This is the reason the editor proposes “Hangul
    Jeongeum” or a new Hangul Standard. Hangul Jeongeum is
    not a mere restoration of the past, but an attempt to
    reinterpret and carry forward the philosophy and structure
    of Hunminjeongeum in accordance with the contemporary
    linguistic environment.
    Hangul Jeongeum transcends its function as a writing system
    and possesses a distinctive structure intrinsically connected to
    the principles of nature, acoustics, and geometric order. In
    this respect, it is fundamentally distinct from existing writing
    systems and carries profound philosophical significance that
    extends beyond conventional frameworks of graphemics.
    In conclusion, Hangul Jeongeum may be described as a truly
    Universal Writing System for all humanity, which embodies
    profound natural philosophy and encompasses the principles
    and philosophy of the universe itself.
