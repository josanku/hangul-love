ㆍ and ㅣ resembling Heaven and Human


    The vowel ㆍ
    serves as the central character
    that connects the eight vowels
    ㅗ ㅏ ㅛ ㅑ ㅜ ㅓ ㅠ ㅕ(o a yo ya u eo yu yeo).


    This reflects how Heaven (ㆍ)
    pervades all things in the world,
    spreading its influence everywhere.


    The addition of Human (ㅣ)
    to the vowels ㅛ ㅑ ㅠ ㅕ(yo ya yu yeo)
    carries deep significance.


    It signifies the unique role of humans
    as the most exceptional beings,
    existing in harmony between Heaven and Earth.
