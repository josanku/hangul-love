    Note

    An interpretation of Hunminjeongeum

    The Hunminjeongeum Haeryebon is a commentary
    published in 1446 that explains why and how King Sejong
    the Great created Hunminjeongeum, Jeongeum or Hangul,
    the Korean script, and how it should be used.
    It is the primary source for understanding the philosophy
    and structure of Hangul. The text was originally written in
    Hanja, or classical Chinese characters.

    “HANGUL, The Cosmic Philosophy” is a translation of this
    with additional comments and gures.
    Rather than providing professional linguistic explanations,
    this work focuses on conveying the principles and
    philosophy behind the creation of Jeongeum from a general
    perspective.

    Here, Jeongeum refers to Hunminjeongeum, but the term
    Hangul, the modern Korean script, is also used for the
    convenience of English speakers. Hangul is the term used
    since 1913 to refer to Jeongeum.

    HANGUL, The Cosmic Philosophy

    Hangul is the universe. The universe is Hangul.
    King Sejong created Hunminjeongeum based on the cosmic
    principles of Hasattang 하사땅 (Samjae, Samtaegeuk, or The
    Three Principle of Hanul, Saram, and Ttang meaning
    Heaven, Human, and Earth) or Hattangsa 하땅사. Hangul
    symbolizes the universe through the combination of
    Wonsene 원세네 (Won, Semo, and Nemo meaning The
    Circle-Triangle-Square trinity: ㅇㅅㅁ) representing
    Hasattang and the geometric elements of ㆍ ㅡ ㅇ (dot, line,
    and circle).
    The logic of the universe is singular, and sound ows
    according to the principles of nature. The universe is
    Hasattang, humanity is Hasattang, and sound is also
    Hasattang.
    Both Hangul consonants and vowels were created under
    the same principles. This is why the shapes of consonants
    resemble vocal organs or the process of pronunciation.
    Hunminjeongeum is a secret code revealing the cosmic
    philosophy, deeper and more fundamental than commonly
    known, embodying the principles of the universe and the
    laws of nature. Moreover, it was designed for anyone to
    understand   and awaken
